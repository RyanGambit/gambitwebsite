Featured in First-Ever Canadian Startup Documentary

Alex Bukowski
December 2025

Gambit's story has been captured in a groundbreaking documentary following Canada's emerging AI companies. The film chronicles the journey of startups that are shaping the future of artificial intelligence north of the border, and Gambit is proud to be featured among them.

A New Lens on Canadian Innovation

The documentary marks the first time a production has focused exclusively on Canada's AI startup ecosystem. It follows the founders, engineers, and teams building the next generation of AI technology from cities across the country.

For Gambit, the feature highlights the company's unique approach to AI workers — purpose-built AI systems that operate as dedicated team members within enterprise environments. The film captures the day-to-day reality of building and deploying these systems at scale.

Why This Matters

Canada has long been recognized as a global leader in AI research, with pioneers like Geoffrey Hinton and Yoshua Bengio laying the foundation for modern deep learning. This documentary shines a light on the next chapter: the companies turning that research into real-world products and services.

Being featured alongside other innovative Canadian startups underscores Gambit's position at the forefront of applied AI, bringing intelligent workers to enterprises that need them most.
