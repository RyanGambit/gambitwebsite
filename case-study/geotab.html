GeoTab Assistant: A Smarter Way to Explore Sponsorship Opportunities

Ryan Burgio
Business Services · GeoTab

The GeoTab Assistant is an AI sponsorship worker launched at GeoTab Connect 2025 to transform how potential sponsors explore partnership opportunities. Instead of static rate cards and back-and-forth emails, the assistant builds customized sponsorship packages in real time based on each brand's objectives, audience, and budget.

Sponsorship sales have traditionally been a manual, relationship-heavy process. The GeoTab Assistant demonstrates that AI can accelerate the early stages of that process without sacrificing the personalization that makes sponsorships effective.

The Challenge

GeoTab Connect is one of the largest telematics conferences in the world, attracting thousands of fleet management professionals and technology partners. The sponsorship opportunities are extensive, ranging from keynote presentations and exhibition space to digital activations and branded experiences. But the sheer volume of options made it difficult for prospective sponsors to quickly identify the packages that aligned with their goals.

The sales team spent significant time fielding exploratory inquiries, walking prospects through menus of options, and manually assembling proposals. Many potential sponsors dropped off before a formal conversation because the initial research phase felt too cumbersome. GeoTab needed a way to engage prospects earlier, qualify their interest faster, and deliver tailored recommendations at the speed of conversation.

The Worker

The GeoTab Assistant engages prospective sponsors in a conversational format, asking targeted questions about their brand objectives, target audience, and investment range. Based on those inputs, it dynamically generates a customized sponsorship package complete with relevant opportunities, estimated reach, and pricing.

  • Dynamic package building: assembles sponsorship bundles in real time from GeoTab Connect's full inventory of opportunities
  • Objective-driven recommendations: tailors suggestions based on whether a sponsor wants brand awareness, lead generation, thought leadership, or product demos
  • Budget-aware: scales recommendations to match stated investment ranges, from emerging partners to headline sponsors
  • Instant delivery: prospects receive a structured proposal within minutes of starting the conversation, not days

The Impact

Launched at GeoTab Connect 2025, the assistant immediately changed the dynamics of sponsorship sales. Prospects who would have spent days in email exchanges instead received personalized proposals during their first interaction. The sales team received higher-quality leads with clear intent signals, allowing them to focus their time on closing rather than qualifying.

The best sponsorship deals happen when a brand sees exactly how their investment maps to their goals. The GeoTab Assistant makes that clarity instant.

The model has proven that AI-driven sponsorship tools can coexist with, and enhance, relationship-based sales. By handling the exploratory phase intelligently, the assistant gives the human sales team a stronger foundation for every conversation that follows.
