Interview: Let's Talk AI with Gambit CTO Chris Silivestru

Ryan Burgio
May 2025

We sat down with Gambit CTO Chris Silivestru and Ellyn Winters-Robinson for a conversation about building scalable AI systems, lessons from Shopify, and what makes Gambit's approach to conversational AI different. The interview was featured on the University of Waterloo AI Institute's "Let's Talk AI" podcast.

On Building AskEllyn

Chris and Ellyn discussed the journey of building AskEllyn, the world's first AI companion for breast cancer support. The project began when Ellyn's book "Flat Please Hold the Shame" caught the attention of Patrick Beliveau, who envisioned an AI tool that could harness the book's insights to fill a significant support gap for breast cancer patients. Chris and the Gambit engineering team brought that vision to life.

On AI Architecture

Chris shared insights from his experience scaling systems at Shopify and how those lessons informed Gambit's cloud architecture. The conversation covered how Gambit builds AI that mirrors human interaction with empathy and nuance — from personality customization to real-time adaptation across languages and contexts.

On AI for Good

The discussion explored the broader potential of conversational AI in healthcare and social impact. AskEllyn has been used in over 50 countries and translated into more than 30 languages, demonstrating the global demand for compassionate, accessible AI support. Chris emphasized that the technical challenge isn't just building smart AI — it's building AI that people trust and genuinely want to engage with.

Looking Ahead

Chris outlined Gambit's vision for the future: conversational AI as the primary platform for engagement, where every conversation starts, enabling humans to do more. He sees AI becoming more integrated into daily life, providing seamless interactions across text, WhatsApp, email, and web — enabling businesses to scale support, optimize operations, and drive deeper engagement.
