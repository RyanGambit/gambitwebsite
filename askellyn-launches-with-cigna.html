AskEllyn Now Live on Cigna Healthcare

Ryan Burgio
September 2025

Gambit is proud to announce that AskEllyn, the world's first AI companion built specifically for breast cancer support, is now live on Cigna Healthcare's patient community platform. This milestone marks a defining moment for both Gambit and the broader healthcare AI landscape, bringing compassionate, always-available support to patients when they need it most.

What Is AskEllyn?

AskEllyn is an AI worker unlike any other. Rather than relying solely on clinical datasets, AskEllyn is grounded in lived experience -- drawing from the real stories, questions, and emotions that breast cancer patients face throughout their journey. From the moment of diagnosis through treatment and recovery, AskEllyn provides personalized guidance, emotional support, and reliable information around the clock.

As an AI worker in Gambit's ecosystem, AskEllyn operates as a dedicated team member within Cigna's patient community. She doesn't replace human care; she extends it, filling the gaps that exist between appointments, after hours, and during the moments of uncertainty that every patient knows too well.

Why Cigna Healthcare

Cigna Healthcare serves millions of patients across the United States and globally. By deploying AskEllyn within Cigna's patient community, Gambit is able to reach breast cancer patients at scale through a platform they already trust. The integration ensures that patients can access AskEllyn seamlessly, without downloading a new app or navigating an unfamiliar interface.

This partnership reflects a shared belief that technology should meet patients where they are. Cigna's commitment to improving whole-person health aligns directly with Gambit's mission to put AI to work in ways that genuinely improve people's lives.

24/7 Support, Grounded in Empathy

Cancer doesn't follow a schedule, and neither does the anxiety that comes with it. AskEllyn is available twenty-four hours a day, seven days a week, providing a consistent and compassionate presence for patients navigating one of the most challenging experiences of their lives. Whether a patient has a question about a symptom at 2 AM or needs reassurance before a procedure, AskEllyn is there.

This launch represents Gambit's continued commitment to building AI workers that are not just intelligent, but deeply human in their purpose. AskEllyn is the proof that AI can be both powerful and personal -- and that enterprise deployment and genuine care are not mutually exclusive.
