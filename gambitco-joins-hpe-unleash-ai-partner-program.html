GambitCo Joins HPE Unleash AI Partner Program

Alex Bukowski
December 2025

GambitCo is proud to announce that we have joined Hewlett Packard Enterprise's Unleash AI Partner Program, a strategic initiative designed to help enterprises operationalize artificial intelligence at scale. This partnership brings Gambit's AI worker platform into HPE's extensive partner ecosystem, giving joint customers a clear path from AI experimentation to production-grade deployment.

The Unleash AI Partner Program represents HPE's commitment to building an ecosystem of best-in-class AI solution providers. By joining the program, Gambit gains access to HPE's global enterprise customer base and deep infrastructure expertise, while HPE's customers gain access to Gambit's purpose-built AI workers that can be deployed directly into business operations.

Why This Partnership Matters

Enterprise AI adoption has reached an inflection point. Most large organizations have completed pilot projects and proven that AI can deliver value. The challenge now is operationalization -- moving from isolated experiments to AI systems that run reliably, securely, and at scale within existing enterprise infrastructure. This is precisely the gap that the Gambit and HPE partnership is designed to close.

HPE brings world-class hybrid cloud and edge computing infrastructure through its GreenLake platform, along with decades of experience serving the world's largest enterprises. Gambit brings AI workers that are designed from the ground up for enterprise environments -- workers that understand business context, respect data governance requirements, and integrate seamlessly into existing workflows. Together, the combination provides a complete stack from infrastructure to intelligence.

Operationalizing AI Across the Enterprise

Through the Unleash AI program, Gambit and HPE will collaborate on joint go-to-market initiatives, solution architectures, and customer engagements. Enterprise customers will be able to deploy Gambit's AI workers on HPE infrastructure with the confidence that comes from two companies deeply committed to security, reliability, and performance. Key areas of focus include:

  • Deploying AI workers for customer-facing operations on HPE GreenLake
  • Building secure, compliant AI solutions for regulated industries
  • Enabling hybrid AI deployments that span cloud and on-premises environments
  • Accelerating time-to-value for enterprise AI initiatives through joint solution blueprints

Looking Forward

Joining HPE's Unleash AI Partner Program is a significant milestone for Gambit. It validates our approach to enterprise AI and positions us alongside leading technology companies that are shaping how businesses adopt and scale artificial intelligence. More importantly, it gives our customers and prospects the confidence that Gambit's AI workers are backed by an infrastructure partner with the scale and reliability that enterprise deployments demand.

We look forward to working closely with HPE to bring intelligent AI workers to enterprises worldwide, helping organizations move beyond AI experimentation and into a future where AI is a core part of how work gets done.
