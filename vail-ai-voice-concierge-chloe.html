Meet Chloe: The Town of Vail's AI Voice Concierge

Alex Bukowski
March 2026

Today, Gambit Co is introducing Chloe, an AI voice concierge deployed for the Town of Vail, Colorado.

Chloe is voice-based. Visitors call (970) 645-VAIL and talk to her the way they'd talk to a local who knows everything about Vail. She pulls real-time parking sensor data and directs callers to the best available spot before they arrive. She knows what events are happening around town on any given day. She speaks 50+ languages. And she picks up every time.

Chloe runs on HPE Private Cloud AI infrastructure powered by NVIDIA, built in partnership with Hewlett Packard Enterprise and Unleash AI partners Vaidio, ProHawk AI, and Kamiwaza.

Chloe is just one piece of a larger effort to bring smart, connected technology to the Town of Vail. Give Chloe a call and see what's possible when technology is built around the visitor experience. She's live now.
