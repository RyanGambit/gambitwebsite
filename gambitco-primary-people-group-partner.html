Primary People Group Becomes First Channel Partner

Ryan Burgio
August 2025

Gambit is excited to announce that Primary People Group has officially become the company's first dedicated channel partner. This partnership marks a pivotal step in Gambit's growth strategy, expanding the reach of its AI workers into new enterprise markets through a trusted and experienced partner.

A Strategic Partnership for Enterprise AI

Primary People Group brings deep expertise in enterprise technology adoption and a strong network of relationships across industries that are actively seeking AI solutions. As Gambit's first channel partner, they will serve as an extension of the Gambit team -- helping organizations understand, evaluate, and deploy AI workers tailored to their specific operational needs.

The partnership is built on a shared conviction that AI adoption in the enterprise requires more than just great technology. It requires guidance, trust, and a partner who understands the complexities of integrating intelligent systems into existing workflows. Primary People Group brings exactly that.

Expanding Gambit's Reach

Until now, Gambit has engaged directly with enterprises to deploy its AI workers. While that approach has driven strong results, the channel partner model opens a new chapter of scale. Primary People Group will introduce Gambit's platform to organizations that may not yet be aware of the possibilities that purpose-built AI workers offer, from customer support and healthcare to operations and beyond.

This model also allows Gambit to stay focused on what it does best -- building and refining AI workers -- while Primary People Group handles relationship development, consultative sales, and client onboarding across new verticals and geographies.

What This Means for Enterprises

For organizations considering AI adoption, working with Primary People Group means access to Gambit's full suite of AI workers through a partner that understands enterprise procurement, compliance, and change management. It lowers the barrier to entry and accelerates time to value, ensuring that companies can move from exploration to deployment with confidence.

This is the first of what Gambit expects to be a growing partner ecosystem, and it signals the company's commitment to making AI workers accessible to every enterprise that needs them.
