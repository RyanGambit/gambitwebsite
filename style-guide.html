01 — Positioning

We build functional AI workers.

Not agents. Not copilots. Not assistants. Workers. with a job title, a scope, a personality, and accountability to an outcome.

The complexity isn't the AI. It's the choreography. A single interaction might touch five workers in sequence before a human ever sees it. That orchestration is the product.

12 — Motion

Purposeful, not decorative. Motion reveals content, not personality.

One easing curve. Short durations. Elements arrive and settle.

Easing

cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)

Duration

150ms hover · 300ms enter · 400ms page

Hover

opacity: 0.75 · 150ms ease

Hover to trigger

Fade in up

Default entrance. 12px travel, 400ms.

translateY(12px) → 0

Modals, tooltips. 200ms.

scale(0.92) → 1

Slide in

Panels, drawers. 350ms.

translateX(-16px) → 0

Stagger

Worker grids, lists. 60ms between siblings.

delay: n × 60ms

×

Never

No bounce. No elastic. No parallax. No auto-play. No color transitions on hover. No spinning loaders.
